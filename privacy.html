Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Overview

PawsAndProof-Uploader ("the Service") is a personal content publishing tool. This privacy policy explains how data is handled when using the Service.

2. Data Collection

The Service does not collect, store, or process any personal data from third-party users. No analytics, cookies, or tracking mechanisms are used.

3. TikTok Account Access

The Service accesses only the channel owner's TikTok account through OAuth 2.0 authentication. The following data is accessed:

  • Authentication tokens: Stored locally on the operator's machine to maintain the login session. Never shared with third parties.
  • Video publishing: The Service uploads video files and metadata (title, description, privacy settings) to the authenticated TikTok account.

4. Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the operator's personal computer and are never transmitted to external servers. No cloud storage or third-party services are used for token storage.

5. Data Sharing

The Service does not share any data with third parties. All operations are between the local machine and TikTok's API servers.

6. Data Retention

Authentication tokens are retained only as long as needed for the Service to function. They can be deleted at any time by the operator.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. No data from children is knowingly collected.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please reach out via the PawsAndProof social media channels.
